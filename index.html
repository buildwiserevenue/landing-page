What style of trading do you focus on?

We primarily focus on Swing Trading using higher timeframes like the Daily and 4-Hour charts. This approach is about identifying quality setups and having the patience to let them play out, making it suitable even for those who can't watch the charts all day.

What is the main goal of the community?

The core goal is to move away from random trading and build a disciplined, process-oriented approach. We achieve this through trade analysis, a focus on psychology, and a supportive community environment where we learn and grow together.

Is this financial advice?

Absolutely not. This is a professional, educational community. All trades and levels are shared on a demo account for hypothetical and learning purposes. The goal is for you to learn a process, not to blindly copy trades.
